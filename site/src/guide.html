Guide

Homepage

Refresh page

To refresh the page, click R.
You usually do not need to press this button because it will automatically do so,
but if it is not working, you can press this button to do it manually.

Create post

To create a post, press the C button.

See your account's page

To see your page, press the U button.

Edit profile

Open the page for your account by pressing U,
then click the E button to edit your bio and PFP.
To change your PFP, use a link directly to an image.

Styling messages

While writing a message, you can use {type,value,value...}
types:
l: link: site, ?name
p: picture: direct link, ?width, ?height
b, i, u, s: bold, italic, underline, strikethrough: (none)
c, h: color, highlight: color
t: toggle: title, text
MORE WILL BE ADDED

To insert emotes, use :emote:
emotes:
mood, goober, horror, nohorror, clueless, silly, roll, mh, moodenheimer,
panic, ralsei, chair, huh, bigshot, sad, alarm, mf, cool

Get rid of "new" bar

Click on the text that says "new".
